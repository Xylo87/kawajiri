- Plot : The film takes place in feudal Japan and follows Kibagami Jubei,
a mercenary swordsman who battles the Eight Devils of Kimon,
a team of ninjas with supernatural powers who are intent on
overthrowing the Tokugawa shogunate.
During his quest, he is aided by Dakuan, an elderly but crafty
government spy, and Kagero, a Kōga kunoichi whose body is infused
with poisonous toxins.
