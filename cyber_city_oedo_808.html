Cyber City Oedo 808
Trailer Original Poster
- Plot : Set in the year 2808 in the city of Oedo (Tokyo),
it tells the story of three criminals who are enlisted into fighting crime
in exchange for reducing their sentences to the point where
they are able to earn their freedom.
The three criminals are Sengoku Shunsuke, an anti-social maverick,
Gogl, a mohawk wearing hacker, and Benten, an androgynous bishōnen.
